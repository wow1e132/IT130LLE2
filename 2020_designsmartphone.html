Best Design Award Smartphone 2020

Galaxy Note 20 Ultra

Good design is good business.

There have been a lot of interesting designs opened to the market this year 2020 but for Marques Brownlee's review, 'Samsung Galaxy Note 20 Ultra' is the winner for this category.

Design and features.

The hardware choices that were inserted in this phone are amazing. The display is also flattened to the front so that it does not curve over to the edges so much. The camera installed in the back also had a laser.
