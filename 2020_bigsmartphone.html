Best Big Smartphone 2020

Xiaomi Mi 10 Ultra

Bulky size.

In selecting the best big smartphone it does not only focus on the size of the phone but also check if it maximizes its use of space. There was tough competition in this category but overall 'Xiaomi Mi 10 Ultra' won.

Bigger. Better.

It maximizes the large space it possesses and provides 120x zoom on the camera, 120Hz display providing smoother motion and transitions, 120-watt fast charging, 50-watt wireless charging, half a TB storage, and so many more top features.
