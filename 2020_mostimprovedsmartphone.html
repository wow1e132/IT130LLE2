Most Improved Smartphone 2020

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 2

Make improvements, not excuses.

It provided a big improvement on the front display from a candy bar-like display to a real usable full-screen phone. Its inside display was also improved with a thinner bezel and 120Hz display.
