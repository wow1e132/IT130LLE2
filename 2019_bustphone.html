Best Bust Smartphone 2019

Google Pixel 4

Unjustifiable price.

The google pixel 4 is not really the worst phone on this desk. We expected a great camera and the latest and greatest google software experience and we did get that, but they started off with a large notch. Later on there were problems on the 90hz screen, there were also battery problems, there is not a very good microphone, and other more. Knowing that the price is at $900, the price was competing with other flagships but what it delivered was not on par with other phones.
