Best Design Smartphone 2019

Samsung Galaxy Fold

Most important design with potential.

The samsung galaxy fold has the most important design of 2019. It is the most interesting phone to come out during the year. The phone gave the possibility of putting a bigger screen on your pocket in the same footprint as other phones. That concept is something to look forward to for the following generations and other companies. The execution was also not bad.
