Best Compact Smartphone 2020

Apple iPhone 12 mini

Lord of the Minis.

It is a bit rare to find a great compact small flagship-level smartphone but reviewing the present compact smartphones in the market, 'iPhone 12 mini' takes the bag for this category.

Cheapest out of all the iPhone 12 series.

It might come as a surprise since iPhone 12 mini is the same as the rest of the iPhone collection but smaller. All the features are the same from the camera, display tech, chip, RAM, software, and pretty much everything but that is the point why iPhone 12 won this category. It has the same features as a regular size phone but it is smaller and easy to carry around.
