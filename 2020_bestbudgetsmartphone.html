Best Budget Smartphone 2020

Google Pixel 4A

Budget-friendly.

This category focuses on a smartphone that gives a high-end experience while having a lower price and the winner is 'Google Pixel 4a'.

Inexpensive phone with a great quality camera.

Provides a high-class camera and a decently well-built phone despite its price and it also has a headphone jack.

Build: Glass front (Gorilla Glass 3), plastic back, plastic frame

Sim: Nano-SIM and/or eSIM

Type: OLED, HDR

Memory
